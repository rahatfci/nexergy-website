Solar Price Discovery at Your Fingertip

You have complete control over your solar purchasing experience on Nexergy. There will be no more door-to-door sellers or unsolicited phone calls. We offer a completely online service that allows you to receive and compare multiple solar quotes before deciding on the best installer for you.

Get Started

STEP 1: ADD YOUR PROPERTY AND GET THE PERSONALIZED ANALYSIS

Using our Solar Calculator, you can easily calculate your solar potential and savings based on your address. Without your permission, we will not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others.

STEP 2: COMPLETE YOUR PROPERTY PROFILE

To get started, it simply takes a few minutes. Enter in your address, energy usage, and your preferences. Our system takes your estimated needs in the marketplace based on your personalized solar analysis.

STEP 3: PRE-SCREENED INSTALLERS SUBMIT QUOTES

To make the quotes thorough and easy to read, they are presented in a consistent manner. You may compare multiple quotes to find the best installers, technology, installation dates, and financing alternatives, such as zero-down leases and loans.

STEP 4: COMPARE QUOTES, PICK THE BEST DEAL, CREATE IMPACT AND SCALE

We give you with all of the information you require to make an informed decision. Choose the best quote from a installer of your choice and rest assured that you are getting a good value.
